Event Description

Welcome to Macedonia SDA Church, a vibrant and welcoming community in Homa Bay Town West Ward, Olodo Village, near Kosike Family! We are thrilled to invite you to our weekly "Worship Services" that take place every Saturday from sunrise to sunset.

At Macedonia SDA Church, we believe in the importance of Sabbath observance, as outlined in the scriptures. In the book of Exodus 20:8-11, God commands His people to remember the Sabbath day and keep it holy. This is a special day set apart for rest, worship, and reflection on God's goodness and creation.

In Isaiah 58:13-14, we are reminded that honoring the Sabbath is a way to find delight in the Lord and receive His blessings. It is a day to set aside our own desires and focus on seeking God's will and delighting in His presence.

The Sabbath is a gift from God, and Jesus Himself emphasized its significance during His earthly ministry. In Mark 2:27, Jesus said, "The Sabbath was made for man, not man for the Sabbath." This reminds us that the Sabbath is a time of rest and spiritual rejuvenation, designed to benefit and bless us.

As Seventh-day Adventists, we hold the Sabbath in high regard and observe it as a day of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Our Worship Services at Macedonia SDA Church, offer a unique opportunity to honor and celebrate the Sabbath in accordance with our beliefs.

During our Worship Services, we explore the scriptures and dive deeper into the teachings of the Bible, including the importance of Sabbath observance. Our knowledgeable and passionate Speakers and Pastor, along with our dedicated Sabbath School teachers, guide us through relevant verses and share insights on how to apply Sabbath principles to our daily lives.

Join us at Macedonia SDA Church every Saturday at 8am, as we gather at our location in Homa Bay Town West Ward, Olodo Village, near Kosike Family, to worship, study the Word of God, and uplift our spirits in a dynamic and inspiring way. Experience the joy of Sabbath observance and the blessings it brings to our lives.

At Macedonia SDA Church, we believe that the Sabbath is a time to connect with God, deepen our faith, and build meaningful relationships with fellow believers. We warmly welcome you to our church family, where you will find friendly faces, warm smiles, and genuine connections as we come together to worship and uplift one another.

So mark your calendars and join us at Macedonia SDA Church every Saturday from sunrise to sunset, as we gather at our church , for our uplifting and inspiring Worship Services. We can't wait to meet you and journey together in faith, as we honor and celebrate the Sabbath according to our Seventh-day Adventist beliefs. See you there!

What you'll Find
  • A vibrant and spirited worship service that includes uplifting music, heartfelt prayers, and inspiring sermons rooted in the teachings of the Bible.
  • Engaging Sabbath School classes for all age groups, providing interactive and immersive learning experiences.
  • A warm and welcoming church family that values inclusivity and fosters a sense of belonging.
  • Interactive Bible study groups for deeper exploration of scripture.
  • Prophecy study sessions to understand and discuss biblical end-time events.
  • Regular song services to uplift and inspire through music
  • Choirs and Choristers team opportunities for those interested in leading worship through song
  • Opportunities for meaningful service within the church and in the wider community, allowing you to make a positive impact on the lives of others.
  • Emphasis on Sabbath observance, with a focus on dedicating this day to rest, worship, and spiritual rejuvenation.
  • Accessible resources and materials for personal Bible study and growth
Requirements
  • Should have a desire to grow spiritually: Members should have a genuine interest in deepening their relationship with God and developing their understanding of biblical principles.
  • Should commit to active participation: To fully benefit from the church, members are expected to actively engage in discussions, prayer sessions, and participate in various activities and initiatives.
Frequently Asked Questions
When and where is the Worship Sabbath schedule to take place?

  • The worship service event is scheduled to take place every Saturday at Macedonia SDA Church Homa Bay West Ward Olodo Village near Kosike Family. Please stay tuned for updates through our official communication channels.

Is there a dress code for the Worship Sabbath Service Event?

  • While there is no strict dress code, we encourage attendees to dress modestly and respectfully for the worship service. The most important thing is to come with a sincere and open heart, ready to worship and connect with others.

Can children attend the Worship Sabbath Service Event?

  • Yes, children are welcome to attend the worship service event. We usually have a dedicated area or program for children where they can engage in age-appropriate activities and teachings. However, we also encourage parents to ensure that their children can participate in the service respectfully and without causing disruption to others.
