Overview
Event Description

Calling all young people! Macedonia SDA Church is thrilled to invite you to our highly anticipated annual Youth Retreat, "Awaken," taking place on September 14, 2023, at Olodo Primary School. This weekend getaway promises to be a life-changing experience filled with engaging workshops, powerful worship sessions, and fun activities that will inspire and equip our youth to navigate the challenges of life while deepening their relationship with God.

At "Awaken: Youth Retreat 2023," our mission is to ignite the faith of our youth and empower them on their spiritual journey. Through a carefully curated program, we will provide an atmosphere of growth, connection, and transformation.

Engaging Workshops: Our retreat will feature a wide range of interactive workshops led by experienced speakers and mentors. These workshops will cover topics that are relevant to the youth's lives, such as identity, purpose, faith in the modern world, overcoming obstacles, and finding strength in adversity. Through these sessions, our young participants will gain valuable insights and practical tools to navigate the challenges they face and emerge stronger in their faith.

Powerful Worship Sessions: Prepare to be moved and inspired during our powerful worship sessions. Led by talented solos, passionate singers, and dynamic speakers, these sessions will create an atmosphere where the youth can freely express their love for God. Through heartfelt prayers, soul-stirring music, and inspiring sermons, we aim to deepen their connection with God and awaken a renewed sense of purpose and devotion.

Fun Activities: In addition to the enriching workshops and worship sessions, we have planned a variety of fun and memorable activities to foster camaraderie and create lasting friendships. From team-building exercises to outdoor adventures, sports competitions, and talent showcases, our retreat will provide a space for the youth to relax, have fun, and build strong bonds with fellow believers.

As we embark on this transformative journey, we are guided by the following Bible verses:

1. "But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God's special possession, that you may declare the praises of him who called you out of darkness into his wonderful light." - 1 Peter 2:9

2. "Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity." - 1 Timothy 4:12

3. "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight." - Proverbs 3:5-6

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to awaken your faith, empower your journey, and forge lifelong connections. Join us at "Awaken: Youth Retreat 2023" and experience a weekend filled with inspiration, growth, and transformation.

What you'll Find
  • Engaging workshops on relevant topics such as identity, purpose, and faith in the modern world.
  • Powerful worship sessions filled with inspiring music, heartfelt prayers, and uplifting sermons.
  • Fun and memorable team-building activities to foster camaraderie and create lasting friendships.
  • Outdoor adventures and sports competitions to promote physical activity and healthy competition.
  • Talent showcases where youth can showcase their skills and talents in a supportive environment.
  • Opportunities for youth to connect with experienced mentors and seek guidance on their spiritual journey.
  • Interactive discussions and Q&A sessions with guest speakers and leaders in the faith community.
  • Time for reflection and personal growth through guided meditation and journaling exercises.
  • Small group sessions for deeper discussions and building a sense of community.
  • Opportunities for youth to serve and make a difference in the local community through volunteer activities.
Requirements
  • Age eligibility: The event is open to youth between the ages of 13 and 18. Participants must provide valid proof of age upon registration.
  • Registration and payment: Interested individuals must complete the online registration form and pay the required registration fee to secure their spot at the event. Payment can be made through event organizers or secure online platform.
  • Code of conduct agreement: All participants are required to adhere to a code of conduct that promotes respect, inclusivity, and positive behavior throughout the duration of the event. Any violation of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action or expulsion from the event without refund. Participants must sign and submit the code of conduct agreement during the registration process.
Frequently Asked Questions
Can parents or guardians accompany their child to the event?

  • Yes, parents or guardians are welcome to attend the event as spectators or chaperones. However, only registered participants are allowed to partake in the activities and competitions.

Is there a dress code associated with the Event?

  • While there is no specific dress code, we recommend participants to dress comfortably and appropriately for physical activities. Closed-toe shoes are highly recommended to ensure safety during any outdoor or sports-related activities.

Are meals provided during the event?

  • Meals are not included in the registration fee. However, there will be food vendors or designated meal breaks where participants can purchase food and drinks. It is also advisable to bring snacks or pack a lunch if preferred. Please note that outside food and beverages may not be allowed in certain designated areas.

Benefits Includes

  • Skill Development: The event offers various activities and competitions that allow youths to develop and enhance their skills in different areas such as sports, arts, or problem-solving. Participating in these activities can help them improve their physical abilities, creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork skills.
  • Networking Opportunities: The event brings together youths from different backgrounds and communities, providing a platform for networking and building new connections. Through interactions with peers and professionals in various fields, participants can expand their social circle, exchange ideas, and potentially find mentors or future collaborators..
  • Self-Confidence Building: Engaging in challenging activities and showcasing their abilities in front of an audience can boost youths' self-confidence. The event provides a supportive and encouraging environment where participants can step out of their comfort zones, overcome obstacles, and gain a sense of accomplishment.
  • Exposure to Diversity: By bringing together youths from diverse backgrounds, the event promotes cultural exchange and understanding. Participants get the opportunity to learn about different cultures, traditions, and perspectives, fostering tolerance, empathy, and respect for others.
  • Leadership Development: In many events, there are opportunities for youths to take on leadership roles, such as team captains, event coordinators, or volunteers. These positions allow them to develop leadership and organizational skills, decision-making abilities, and learn how to effectively work with a team.
  • Inspiration and Motivation: Attending the event and witnessing the skills and achievements of other participants can inspire and motivate youths to pursue their own goals and aspirations. They can learn from the experiences and success stories of others, igniting a sense of determination and perseverance to overcome challenges and work towards their dreams.
