Rise and Shine: Empowered Women, Empowered World

Celebrating the Strength of Women, Embracing Faith, and Cultivating Lifelong Friendships

Join us on 24/09/2023 at Macedonia SDA Church for "Rise and Shine: Empowered Women, Empowered World." This event celebrates the strength and achievements of women, inspiring personal growth and fostering lifelong friendships. Together, we will shape a brighter, more inclusive future. Don't miss this transformative experience that will leave an indelible mark on the world. Rise and shine with us at Macedonia SDA Church.

Various activities

24th Sept 2023 ~ 8am -6pm

40 women enrolled

Overview
Event Description

"Empowered Women, Empowered World: A Journey of Faith and Friendship" is an upcoming luncheon event that aims to celebrate the strength and resilience of women, drawing inspiration from the teachings of the Bible. As we eagerly anticipate this gathering, let us look to Proverbs 31:25, which says, "She is clothed with strength and dignity, and she laughs without fear of the future." This luncheon will provide a space for women to embrace their God-given strength and find encouragement in their faith.

In this journey of faith and friendship, we will explore the power of community and the importance of supporting one another. Ecclesiastes 4:9-10 reminds us, "Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor: If either of them falls down, one can help the other up." Let us eagerly anticipate the opportunity to come together, uplifting each other and celebrating the unique gifts and talents that each woman brings.

During the luncheon, we will have the privilege of hearing inspiring testimonies from women who have experienced transformation in their lives. Romans 12:2 encourages us, "Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind." Their stories will serve as a powerful reminder of God's love and the strength that comes from surrendering to His will.

Engaging in interactive workshops and group discussions, we will be guided by the words of Proverbs 27:17, which says, "As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another." Through these activities, we will have the opportunity to sharpen and refine our faith, gaining new insights and perspectives that will empower us in our daily lives.

As we gather, this luncheon will provide a unique opportunity to connect with one another on a deeper level. Colossians 3:14 reminds us, "And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity." Let us eagerly anticipate exemplifying the love of Christ as we build meaningful friendships and support one another in our personal journeys.

Moments of reflection and prayer will be an integral part of our time together. Philippians 4:6 reminds us, "Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God." Through prayer, we will find solace, strength, and guidance, knowing that we can trust in God's unfailing love.

As we enjoy a delectable lunch, let us remember the example set by Jesus, who often shared meals with his disciples and others. In Luke 10:38-42, we learn about the importance of hospitality and attentiveness to others. May our time together be marked by warmth, hospitality, and a spirit of generosity.

Laughter and joy will fill the atmosphere as we engage in fun activities. Proverbs 17:22 reminds us, "A cheerful heart is good medicine, but a crushed spirit dries up the bones." Through joy and laughter, we can experience healing and renewal, lifting our spirits and fostering a positive environment.

Forgiveness and reconciliation will be emphasized during this luncheon. In Matthew 6:14-15, Jesus teaches us, "For if you forgive other people when they sin against you, your heavenly Father will also forgive you. But if you do not forgive others their sins, your Father will not forgive your sins." Let us eagerly anticipate embracing forgiveness and seeking reconciliation, cultivating an environment of love and grace.

Mark your calendars, spread the word, and be a part of Macedonia SDA Church's Women Empowerment. Together, As we look forward to this upcoming event, let us carry the message of empowerment, faith, and friendship with us. Galatians 3:28 reminds us, "There is neither Jew nor Gentile, neither slave nor free, nor is there male and female, for you are all one in Christ Jesus." Let us eagerly anticipate empowering and uplifting one another, knowing that in Christ, we are all equal and valuable members of God's family.

What you'll Find
  • Inspirational speakers sharing their stories of strength and resilience.
  • Interactive workshops and discussions on topics like self-care, confidence-building, and goal-setting.
  • Opportunities to connect with like-minded women and build lifelong friendships.
  • Prayer and reflection sessions to deepen spiritual connection and find guidance.
  • A delicious lunch spread to enjoy while fostering a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
  • Engaging activities and games designed to promote laughter and joy.
  • Emphasis on forgiveness and reconciliation, encouraging healing and growth.
  • An inclusive and supportive environment that celebrates diversity and unity.
  • A renewed sense of purpose, inspiration, and motivation to pursue dreams and make a positive impact in the world.
Requirements
  • Registration: Attendees must register in advance to secure their spot at the luncheon event. This requirement ensures that the organizers can adequately prepare for the number of attendees, plan seating arrangements, and provide a memorable experience for everyone involved. Registration may involve filling out a form, providing contact information, and possibly making a payment for the event.
  • Respectful and Inclusive Behavior: All attendees must adhere to a code of conduct that promotes a respectful and inclusive environment. This requirement ensures that everyone feels safe, valued, and heard during the event. Attendees are expected to treat others with kindness, empathy, and respect, regardless of their background, beliefs, or personal circumstances.
  • Openness to Faith and Spiritual Exploration: While the event welcomes women from all walks of life, attendees should have an open mind and be willing to engage in discussions and activities that incorporate faith and spiritual exploration. The event aims to celebrate the strength of women through the lens of biblical teachings and values.
Frequently Asked Questions
Can I attend the Event if I do not identify as a religious or do not follow a specific faith?

  • Absolutely! The event welcomes women from all backgrounds, regardless of their religious beliefs or affiliations. While the event incorporates faith and spiritual exploration, it is designed to be inclusive and open-minded. Attendees are encouraged to engage in discussions and activities with an open heart and mind, regardless of their personal beliefs.

Is there a dress code for the Event?

  • There is no specific dress code for the event. However, we recommend business casual attire to maintain a respectful and professional atmosphere. Participants are encouraged to dress comfortably while keeping in mind the importance of creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all attendees.

Can I bring a friend or a family member to the Event?

  • Yes, you are welcome to bring a friend or family member to the event! We encourage attendees to spread the word and invite others who may be interested in the theme and purpose of the event. However, please note that each attendee must register individually to secure their spot, as space is limited. We recommend registering early to ensure that both you and your guest can attend the event together.
