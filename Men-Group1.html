Brotherhood Builders

Biblical Manhood: Building Strong Foundations

Join our group of like-minded men as we explore the principles of biblical manhood, focusing on developing strong foundations in faith, family, and community. Together, we'll dive into scripture, share experiences, and support one another in our journey towards becoming godly men.

12+ Lesson

4 -5pm Thursdays

32 men enrolled

Overview
Group Description

Welcome to "Brotherhood Builders" at Macedonia SDA Church! Our group is dedicated to exploring the principles of biblical manhood and building strong foundations in faith, family, and community. We believe that as men, we have a significant role to play in our homes, workplaces, and society, and this group aims to support and encourage one another on this journey.

In "Brotherhood Builders," we dive deep into scripture, seeking wisdom and guidance on what it means to be a godly man. Through interactive discussions, Bible studies, and shared experiences, we explore topics such as integrity, leadership, fatherhood, relationships, and personal growth. We believe that by grounding ourselves in the Word of God, we can become the men He has called us to be.

This group provides a safe and non-judgmental space for men to share their struggles, triumphs, and insights. We understand that each individual's journey is unique, and we value the diversity of experiences and perspectives within our group. Together, we strive to support one another, offering guidance, accountability, and friendship.

"Brotherhood Builders" also aims to extend our impact beyond our immediate circle. We actively seek opportunities for community service, reaching out to those in need and making a positive difference in our local community. By living out our faith through acts of service, we hope to inspire others and be a shining example of Christ's love.

Whether you're a young man just starting your journey or a seasoned individual with years of experience, "Brotherhood Builders" welcomes you with open arms. Join us as we grow together in faith, encourage one another, and make a lasting impact on the world around us.

We meet regularly, both in person and online, to accommodate various schedules and preferences. We look forward to getting to know you and embarking on this exciting journey of biblical manhood together!

If you have any questions or would like more information, please don't hesitate to reach out. We can't wait to have you join our "Brotherhood Builders" group at Macedonia SDA Church!

What you'll learn
  • The importance of biblical manhood and its impact on personal and spiritual growth.
  • Strategies for building strong foundations in faith, family, and community.
  • How to cultivate integrity and live out godly principles in everyday life.
  • Effective leadership skills and qualities that can be applied in various contexts.
  • Nurturing healthy and fulfilling relationships with spouses, children, and others.
  • Practical tips for being a supportive and loving father.
  • Tools for personal growth and self-improvement, both spiritually and emotionally.
  • The power of accountability and how it can help in overcoming challenges.
  • Understanding and embracing the unique roles and responsibilities of men in society.
  • Applying biblical principles to navigate work-life balance and career success
Requirements
  • Must be male: The group is specifically designed for men who are seeking to grow in their faith and embrace biblical manhood.
  • Must have a desire to grow spiritually: Members should have a genuine interest in deepening their relationship with God and developing their understanding of biblical principles.
  • Must commit to active participation: To fully benefit from the group, members are expected to actively engage in discussions, attend meetings, and participate in group activities and initiatives.
Frequently Asked Questions
How often will the instructor be available for guidance and support?

  • The instructor is committed to being available for guidance and support throughout the duration of the program. They will be accessible through regular meetings, email correspondence, and even phone calls if needed. The instructor understands the importance of timely support and will make every effort to be there for the members.

What topics will the instructor cover during the program?

  • The instructor will cover a wide range of topics that are relevant to men's spiritual growth and biblical manhood. Some of the key areas of focus may include understanding one's identity in Christ, developing healthy relationships, overcoming challenges and temptations, leadership, and embracing God's purpose for their lives. The instructor will tailor the content to meet the specific needs and interests of the group

Can the Instructor provide resources or recommendations for further studies?

  • Absolutely! The instructor is well-versed in various resources, books, and materials that can further enhance the learning experience. They will gladly provide recommendations and share valuable resources with the members. Whether it's books, podcasts, devotionals, or online courses, the instructor will strive to equip the members with tools to deepen their understanding and continue their growth beyond the program.
