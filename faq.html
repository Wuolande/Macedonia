Most frequently asked questions

Here are the most frequently asked questions you may check before getting started

What are the service times for Macedonia SDA Church?

Our regular service times are as follows:

  • Sabbath School: 8:00 AM
  • Devine Service: 11:30 AM
  • Children Service: 2:00 PM
  • Song Service: 3.00 PM
  • Bible Study/Prophecy: 4:00 PM
  • Wednesday Vespers: 5:00 PM
  • Friday Vespers: 6.00 PM
  • Prayer Sessions: Everyday @6.00 AM
    • How can I get involved in the church community?

    We encourage everyone to engage in our various ministries and activities. You can join our Sabbath School classes, participate in community outreach programs, become a part of our music ministry, or volunteer for different church events.

    Feel free to reach out to us for more information on how to get involved!

    Is there a children's ministry at Macedonia SDA Church?

    Yes, we have an active children's ministry that caters to different age groups. We provide Sabbath School classes, children's church, and special events throughout the year to nurture the spiritual growth of our young ones.

    How can I donate to Macedonia SDA Church?

    We appreciate your willingness to support our church. You can make donations in person during our services, or you can visit our website's "Donations" page to contribute online.

    We accept various forms of payment, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment apps.

    Are there any upcoming events or special programs?

    Yes, we regularly organize special events and programs for our church community. You can stay updated by visiting our website's "Events" page or subscribing to our newsletter.

    We also announce upcoming events during our services and through our social media channels.

    How can I contact the church office for further inquiries?

    We are always here to assist you! You can reach out to our church office by phone at 0742944297 during office hours. Additionally, you can send us an email at admin@macedoniasdachurch.co.ke, and we will respond to your inquiries as soon as possible.

    At Macedonia SDA Church, we are dedicated to serving our community and spreading the love of Christ. Our mission is to provide a place where individuals can grow in their faith, find meaningful connections, and make a positive impact in the world. Through worship, fellowship, and outreach, we strive to reflect the teachings of Jesus Christ and share His message of hope, love, and salvation. Join us on this journey as we seek to make a difference in the lives of those around us, and together, let us shine the light of Christ in our community. Welcome to Macedonia SDA Church, where you are embraced as part of God's family.
