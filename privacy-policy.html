Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23rd of June, 2023

Collection of Personal Information

Our data privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect from individuals. This may include but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact information, and demographic data. We ensure that the collection of personal information is done lawfully, with the consent of the individual, and for specific purposes outlined in our policy.

Use of Personal Information

We use the personal information collected from individuals to provide the requested services, products, or information. This may include processing contributions, responding to inquiries, personalizing user experiences, and improving our offerings. We make it clear to individuals how their personal information will be used and ensure that it is used only for the purposes specified.

Sharing of Personal Information.

We understand the importance of maintaining the privacy of personal information and are committed to not sharing it with third parties unless necessary. In cases where sharing is required, we ensure that proper safeguards are in place to protect the information and that it is only shared with trusted partners who adhere to similar privacy standards.

Security Measures

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. This includes the use of encryption, firewalls, secure storage, and regular monitoring to detect and address any potential security breaches.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in our data privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Once the information is no longer needed, we ensure its secure deletion or anonymization to protect the privacy of individuals.

User Rights and Choices

We respect the rights of individuals to control their personal information. Our data privacy policy outlines the rights users have, including the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information. We provide options for individuals to manage their preferences and communication settings, giving them control over how their information is used and shared.

At Macedonia SDA Church, we are dedicated to serving our community and spreading the love of Christ. Our mission is to provide a place where individuals can grow in their faith, find meaningful connections, and make a positive impact in the world. Through worship, fellowship, and outreach, we strive to reflect the teachings of Jesus Christ and share His message of hope, love, and salvation. Join us on this journey as we seek to make a difference in the lives of those around us, and together, let us shine the light of Christ in our community. Welcome to Macedonia SDA Church, where you are embraced as part of God's family.
